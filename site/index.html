macOS menu bar app

Scroll back through the last few minutes of your screen.

JustNow quietly captures your recent screen history so you can pull up a fullscreen timeline whenever you need to recover a detail you have just moved past.

Download the latest release Read release notes

Fast recall

Open the timeline with a hotkey and scrub backwards through your recent capture history without digging through folders or screenshots.

Battery-aware capture

Capture cadence, indexing, and retention adapt to battery and thermal conditions so the app stays practical as a background tool.

Built for the menu bar

JustNow lives in the menu bar, keeps its controls close, and stays out of the way until you need a quick trip back in time.

Requirements

macOS 15 or later with Screen Recording permission granted.

Distribution

Signed and notarised builds are published on GitHub Releases.

Updates

The public app page and Sparkle appcast live in this repo. Repository builds already include in-app update support, and stable public releases are served from /appcast.xml.
